    Maple Cottage is part of a luxurious development of holiday cottages on the quiet village road of Radstone with stunning views and excellent facilities. The cottages are located within a working farm in a secluded location backing onto an antient woodland and rolling fields offering guests contemporary living finished to a high specification, making it a perfect spot to kick back and relax with great facilities at arm's reach (see below).

    Briary Cottages @ Iletts Farm is situated north of Brackley a historic market town dating back to 1086 built on the intersection trading routes between London, Birmingham, Cambridge and Oxford. Brackley is close to Silverstone home of British Motor Racing and home to the Mercedes AMG Petronas F1 team. Further afield, there are a number of National Trust places to visit including: Stowe, Waddesdon Manor, Claydon House and Cannon Ashby or Sulgrave Manor the birthplace of George Washington’s family, Bicester Heritage Centre, Evenly Wood Garden and Blenheim Palace the ancestral home of Winston Churchill.

    If you’re looking for a bit of retail therapy there’s no better place to go than Bicester Village offering luxury and designer goods at discounted prices, the historic city of Oxford with its new Westgate shopping centre or Milton Keynes.

    For the more active Brackley Leisure Centre offers a fantastic range of facilities including a new 25m pool, dedicated learner pool and 110 station gym. Parkrun’s are held in Buckingham (8mile away).

    Families will love the enclosed location its locality to Warwick Castle, Harry Potter in Oxford, Cotswold Wildlife Park, Woburn Safari Park, Milton Keynes Snowdome, Rookery Open Farm, Grand Union cancel and canal museum, Buckinghamshire Railway Centre along with the numerus museums, gardens, cinemas, bowling alleys, climbing walls and trampoline centres to visit.

    In summary, the combination of the superb facilities and the fantastic surroundings will guarantee a memorable stay that couples and families will return to year after year.

    Maple Cottage is the third of our two bedroom units and has been designed for wheel chair access in mind. There is dedicated parking for blue badge holders towards the far left of the entrance area, a large flat area allowing easy access to the cottages. There are rural views to the rear of the property enjoyed from the french doors of the large open plan lounge/dining area. The open plan layout creates a spacious feel and boasts a modern, well equipped kitchen; a dining/lounge area with stylish Dormy House furniture ; smart TV & DVD player and dining table seating 4.

    The two bedrooms lead from the spacious hallway and front door which is easily accessed from the adjacent parking area.

    Outside there is a screened patio area with outdoor furniture, barbecue and lawn.
